Tic-Tac-Toe

Who wants to play a game of tic-tac-toe? Do you feel like you have what it takes to win? The only way to find out, is to play!

    Here's how to play!
    Figure out if you want to first or second.
    If you are first then you will be player one and will play as a square
    If you are second, then you will play as a circle.
    First player to get 3 in row going horizontally and or vertically will be the winner!
    Have fun playing tic-tac-toe!
